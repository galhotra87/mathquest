THE METHOD

How learning happens (the right way)

Unlike rote drilling, MathQuest follows a research-backed 3-step method proven to build lasting math intuition.

1

LEARN — Trick first, then theory

Each topic begins with 15 short, kid-friendly lessons that teach the contest tricks (e.g., "pair-walking" to count factors, "all-of-one" trick for coin counting, "working backwards" for word problems). Lessons use real examples — not abstract formulas.

2

PRACTICE — Apply the trick on olympiad-style questions

After lessons, your child practices 25 carefully chosen questions per level (Foundation, Intermediate, Advanced). Every question is in the style of major math competitions and olympiads.

3

MASTER — Step-by-step feedback after every question

Right answers get a celebration. Wrong answers get a kid-friendly multi-step explanation showing exactly how to think through the problem. Mistakes become learning moments, not frustration.
